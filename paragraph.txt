The purpose of a paragraph is to express a speakers thoughts on a particular point in a clear way that is unique and specific to that paragraph . In other words  , paragraphs shouldn't be mixing thoughts or idea . When a new idea is introduce , generally , a writer will introduce a new paragraph . Paragraphs provide structure and flow to your text . And random paragraph allow you to move from one thought to another . When you start a new paragraph you are telling your reader that the topic is over and you are moving on . Without this structure , your brilliant ideas and your sound argumentation will be difficult to follow .
